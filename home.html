Description

Gamblers Gambit, a game that aims to demonstrate to players how gambling is a societal issue and that it can affect anyone due to its nature of risk, chance, and prize. The Objective of the game is to teach primarily young children to never get addicted to the horrible habit of gambling. Gambit began as a game that a bunch of kids made in school as a project but then evolved to much more. Gamblers gambit is now widely known around the U.S. These kids are now full time working on this board game that has grown to become more of a teaching tool then a game.
