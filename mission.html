Our Mission

The Social Impact: Our Commitment to Prevent Gambling Addiction



Mission Statement

The mission of our game is to provide a fun household game that teaches young kids in adolescence about gambling and how addicting it could be. We have rigged the game to have unfair outcome that usually lead to loss similar to a casino to make sure the correct message gets out.

Impact of The Gambler's Gambit Board Game

Its board game represents how different places can change quickly. One space can be an easy bet to win low cash but low risk and the next could be a high risk high reward.

Vision Statement

The vision of Gamblers gambit is that people tend to see the plus side without examining the bad being tunnel visioned to the rewards.

Looking to the Future

The future of our game is to grow a game for future kids to me involved in the right way to success and to grow an anti gambling mentality to stop gambling.
